About Me

Hi, I'm Pranoy.

I'm a 15-year-old from Warren, NJ who turned a coding hobby into a real business. I build websites, apps, and automation tools for local businesses — and I genuinely love every minute of it.

My Story

From Side Project to Purpose

It started casually — building a website here, an app there, just because I enjoyed it. I'd spend nights and weekends coding, not because anyone asked me to, but because I genuinely love building things that work.

Then something clicked. I saw local businesses struggling with the same problems over and over — paying too much for generic solutions that didn't fit, or just going without any tech at all. I realized the skills I'd been honing as a hobby could actually make a real difference for real people.

So I turned Prism into something more than a side project. Now it's my way of helping local businesses get the same quality tech that big companies have — without the big-company price tag. I'm not in this to get rich. I'm in this because I genuinely want to help.
