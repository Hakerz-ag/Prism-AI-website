About Me

Your Local Tech Partner

I'm the person behind Prism — a one-person agency on a mission to bring enterprise-level technology to local businesses at prices that actually make sense.

My Story

From Frustration to Innovation

I started Prism because I saw a problem that nobody was solving. Local businesses — the restaurants, taxi companies, camps, and insurance agencies that keep our communities running — were being left behind by the tech revolution.

They were paying too much for too little. Cookie-cutter templates that didn't fit. Enterprise solutions priced way out of reach. And worst of all, no one to call when things broke.

So I built Prism to be different. I don't just deliver a product and disappear — I become your long-term tech partner. I learn your business inside and out, build exactly what you need, and stick around to make sure it keeps working.
